PNP — Provincial Nominee Programs

Each Canadian province and territory runs its own immigration program targeting workers in occupations they need most. A provincial nomination adds 600 CRS points — virtually guaranteeing an ITA in the next Express Entry draw.

Atlantic & Rural

AIP & RCIP

Atlantic Immigration Program (AIP) is a pathway for skilled workers and international graduates who want to live in one of Canada's four Atlantic provinces: New Brunswick, Nova Scotia, PEI, or Newfoundland.

Rural and Northern Immigration Pilot (RCIP) connects skilled workers with smaller communities across Canada that need workers but don't receive high immigration volumes.

Employer-driven — job offer often required
Lower competition vs. Express Entry
Permanent residence pathway directly
Great for healthcare, trades, and service workers
