The National Innovation Visa

Australia’s premier route for exceptional, internationally recognised talent — founders, researchers, investors and global leaders shaping the industries of tomorrow.

The Visa

A permanent home for the world’s exceptional talent.

The National Innovation Visa is a permanent residence pathway reserved for individuals with an internationally recognised record of exceptional and outstanding achievement. It targets people whose work elevates Australia’s standing in priority sectors — from advanced technology and research to entrepreneurship and investment.

This is not a points-tested visa. Selection is evidence-led: it rewards demonstrable impact, recognition by peers, and the capacity to contribute at the highest level. Truvia positions your achievements with the rigour and narrative such a profile demands.

Indicators of High Achievement

What “exceptional” looks like in evidence.

No single factor defines eligibility. The department weighs a body of evidence demonstrating sustained, internationally recognised achievement and the prospect of continued contribution in Australia.

01
International Recognition
Awards, honours and distinctions acknowledged beyond your home country.
02
Industry Impact
Measurable influence — revenue, adoption, citations, or sector-shaping outcomes.
03
Peer Standing
Endorsement from leaders, institutions and recognised bodies in your field.
04
Innovation Contributions
Patents, products, publications or ventures that advance the state of the art.
05
Leadership
Senior roles, board positions, and a track record of guiding teams or organisations.

Frequently Asked

Innovation Visa questions

Is the National Innovation Visa points-tested?+
No. Unlike General Skilled Migration, this visa is assessed on the strength of your evidence — your record of exceptional, internationally recognised achievement and your potential to keep contributing in Australia, rather than a points score.
What sectors are prioritised?+
Australia focuses on areas of national strategic interest — advanced technology, research and innovation, entrepreneurship and investment among them. Aligning your profile with a priority sector strengthens your case.
Do I need a job offer or sponsor?+
A formal employer sponsor is not the basis of this visa. Instead, the focus is on your standing and impact. Recognition and endorsement from credible individuals and institutions in your field play an important supporting role.
How does Truvia help with such an application?+
We specialise in turning a complex career into a clear, evidence-led narrative — identifying your strongest achievements, curating supporting documentation, and presenting a coherent case that meets the high bar this visa sets. Every profile is assessed individually.
