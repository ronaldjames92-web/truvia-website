Your Pathway

From qualification to PR.

Most engineers assess through Engineers Australia's Competency Demonstration Report or Washington Accord recognition, then pursue GSM (189/190/491) or employer sponsorship (482/186) if already holding a job offer.

Frequently Asked

Engineer PR questions

Which engineering disciplines qualify?+
Civil, mechanical, electrical, chemical, mining, industrial and software engineering all appear on the MLTSSL. We confirm your exact occupation code before starting.
Do I need a Washington Accord-recognised degree?+
It simplifies things, but it's not mandatory — engineers with other qualifications can assess via the Competency Demonstration Report, documenting their skills against the required standard.
