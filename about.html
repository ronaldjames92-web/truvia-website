Company Profile

Who we are

Truvia Consultants is Kuwait's leading immigration consultancy, founded with a single mission: to make the process of building a new life abroad honest, clear, and achievable for every client.

Our team of MARA-registered immigration consultants and case managers specialises in Australia skilled migration — the most sought-after destination for professionals leaving the Gulf.

We are fully bilingual in Arabic and English, and operate from our office in Salmiya, Kuwait — serving clients across Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, UAE, and the wider GCC.
